AI-Generated Home Activities

Tailored suggestions to extend Leo's learning at home

Building Together

At home, invite Leo to build with you. Take turns adding pieces. When his structure falls, model saying: “That’s okay! Let’s think about why it fell and try differently.” This builds frustration tolerance in a safe setting.

Social-Emotional Motor Skills

Feelings Vocabulary

Read stories about characters who feel frustrated or angry (e.g., “When Sophie Gets Angry”). After reading, ask: “Have you ever felt like Sophie? What did you do?” Create a “feelings chart” together.

Nature Explorer

Take Leo on nature walks with a magnifying glass. Ask open-ended questions: “What do you notice about this leaf? Why do you think the ant is carrying that crumb?” This builds scientific inquiry and verbal expression.
