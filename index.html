12-Month Roadmap

Sandbox Implementation Timeline

A phased approach from development through pilot to national scaling, designed for rigorous evaluation and sustainable impact.

Months 1–3

Development & AI Foundation

Deploy cloud infrastructure on Azure SG region. Build the four core modules (Planner, Portfolio, Coach, Dashboard). Engineer and validate AI prompts for QTT/SFw alignment using GPT-4.1-mini via Azure OpenAI. Implement PDPA compliance framework and role-based access control.

Months 4–5

Pilot Deployment & Onboarding

Onboard 2–3 pilot centres. Conduct educator onboarding workshops and digital literacy training. Establish baseline metrics for documentation time, lesson quality, and coaching frequency. Complete security audit and penetration testing.

Months 6–9

Active Classroom Usage & Iteration

Live platform usage across pilot centres. Generate SFw-aligned Individual Development Plans (IDPs). Run weekly AI coaching sessions. Collect iterative feedback for continuous improvement. Refine AI models and user experience based on real-world data.

Months 10–12

Impact Assessment & Scaling

Conduct end-of-pilot QTT assessment. Measure quantitative time-saved metrics against baseline. Administer educator satisfaction surveys. Consolidate findings into comprehensive impact report. Present outcomes and scaling proposal to ECDA. Prepare for national rollout across Busy Bees centres.
