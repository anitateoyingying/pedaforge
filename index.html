The Challenge

Why Singapore's ECE Sector Needs PedaForge

Early childhood educators face a documentation crisis that siphons time, energy, and passion away from the children they serve.

⚠
8hrs/week

Emotional Exhaustion

Educators spend up to 8 hours per week on administrative documentation tasks. Research consistently links this burden to emotional exhaustion, reduced job satisfaction, and higher attrition rates in the ECE sector.

⚙
35%

Pedagogical Compromise

Less time available for individualised interactions means 35% fewer opportunities for differentiated learning. One-size-fits-all lesson planning fails to address diverse developmental profiles in each classroom.

Siloed

Siloed Leadership

Directors lack real-time visibility into pedagogical quality across their centres. Professional appraisals remain subjective, infrequent, and disconnected from evidence-based frameworks like QTT and SFw.

6-Month Roadmap

Sandbox Implementation Timeline

A focused approach from architecture to impact assessment, designed for rapid delivery and rigorous evaluation.

Months 1–2

Development & AI Foundation

Deploy cloud infrastructure on Azure SG region. Develop the four core modules. Engineer and validate AI prompts for QTT/SFw alignment. Implement PDPA compliance framework and role-based access control.

Month 3

Pilot Deployment

Onboard 2–3 pilot centres. Conduct educator onboarding workshops and digital literacy training. Establish baseline metrics for documentation time, lesson quality, and coaching frequency. Security audit and penetration testing.

Months 4–5

Active Classroom Usage

Live platform usage across pilot centres. Generate first SFw-aligned Individual Development Plans (IDPs). Run weekly coaching sessions. Collect iterative feedback for continuous improvement.

Month 6

Impact Assessment & Reporting

Conduct end-of-pilot QTT assessment. Measure quantitative time-saved metrics against baseline. Administer educator satisfaction surveys. Consolidate findings into comprehensive impact report. Present outcomes and scaling proposal to ECDA.
